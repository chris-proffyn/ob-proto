Place bank logo images here named as:

- monzo.png
- nationwide.png
- lloyds.png

The app loads logos from /logos/<bank-slug>.png where <bank-slug> is the lowercased bank name with non-alphanumerics replaced by dashes.

Example:
- Bank Name: "Lloyds" -> /logos/lloyds.png
- Bank Name: "Nationwide" -> /logos/nationwide.png
- Bank Name: "Monzo" -> /logos/monzo.png

Recommended sizes:
- 128x128 or 256x256 PNG with transparent background.
